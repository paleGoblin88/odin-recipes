Beef Miso Ramen Recipe

Beef miso ramen is a dish that consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a soup made with beef broth and miso paste. The soup is typically flavored with soy sauce, sake, and mirin, and may include ingredients such as sliced beef, green onions, and mushrooms. It is a variation of traditional Japanese ramen, which is usually made with pork-based broth.
